BorePay

Our mission

BorePay is a tech startup whose product streamlines the payment process for borehole maintenance fees, making it more affordable and accessible for rural communities. Our mission is to ensure a sustainable and reliable water supply for these communities.

About our product

Our digital admin platform and RFID-enabled borehole attachment facilitate easy collection of maintenance fees, streamlined communication with users, track borehole usage, and restrict usage based on specified parameters. The platform also gathers valuable insights and data that can be used to improve community water sanitation.

Borehole attachment device

To be installed at the borehole site, constantly monitored by the BorePay agent.

RFID cards

Each family is issued a card for accessing the borehole at any time of their need. The card's status is cleared whenever one deposits their monthly fee for borehole maintenance..

BorePay agent interface

The former borehole custodian will now be a BorePay agent. They can onboard community members and manage payments via this seamless interface on their smart phone
